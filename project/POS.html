GUI Application: POS - Point of Sale Application

Class Project | Spring 2018

Feb-May 2018 | Long Beach, CA

image of the final product here !

OVERVIEW

POS: point of sale is a Java GUI application to help streamline restaurant procedures such as tracking inventory, processing trasactions, adding menu items, and tracking hours worked by employees. Employees will have the ability to process transactions through the sales interface and will also be able to clock in and out of work from the time clock interface. Managers will be able to view, add and update food inventory through the inventory interface. They also will be able to add and update employee information through the control panel interface. For full documentation and source code, please visit github.
